Who We Are

The Lady Rich Glorious Light Foundation is a Nigerian non-governmental organisation committed to uplifting lives through education support, empowerment programmes, and strategic grant disbursement.

We believe every person — regardless of gender, age, or background — deserves the opportunity to thrive.

What We Do

Education Support

We provide scholarships, school supplies, and academic mentorship to students who need a helping hand to reach their full potential.

Empowerment

We equip individuals with vocational skills, financial literacy, and entrepreneurial tools — empowering everyone to become self-sufficient and contribute to their communities.

Grant Disbursement

We partner with and fund credible NGOs whose work aligns with our mission, multiplying our impact across Nigeria and beyond.

Transparency

We operate with full openness and accountability to our beneficiaries and the public.

Impact

Every programme and grant is designed to create measurable, lasting change in people's lives.

Sustainable Development

We invest in long-term solutions, not short-term fixes, building communities that endure.
