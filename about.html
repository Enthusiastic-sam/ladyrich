About the Foundation

A glorious light for every Nigerian

Our Story

The Lady Rich Glorious Light Foundation was established on July 2nd, 2025, by Her Excellency, Amb. Dr Obodo Rachel — affectionately known as Lady Rich — with a singular, powerful conviction: that every Nigerian deserves the chance to live a life of dignity, purpose, and prosperity.

Born out of a deep passion for community development and human upliftment, the Foundation was created to be more than an organisation — it was created to be a movement. A movement rooted in the belief that when we empower one person, we strengthen an entire community; and when we strengthen communities, we build a nation.

From its base in Abakaliki, Ebonyi State, the Foundation reaches across Nigeria, extending support, opportunity, and hope to individuals, families, and organisations working tirelessly for a better tomorrow.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals, communities, and organisations across Nigeria through education, grants, and skills development — fostering a society built on transparency, impact, and sustainable growth.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every person has equal access to opportunity — where no potential is wasted, no community is left behind, and every light shines as brightly as it was meant to.

What We Stand For

Inclusivity

We empower everyone — men, women, youth, the elderly — regardless of gender, tribe, or background.

Accountability

Every naira disbursed is tracked, reported, and accounted for. Our beneficiaries and donors deserve nothing less.

Community First

Our programmes are shaped by the real needs of the communities we serve — designed with them, not just for them.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in every programme, partnership, and interaction.

Compassion

Behind every grant and programme is a genuine desire to see lives transformed and families uplifted.

Collaboration

We believe the best solutions emerge when governments, NGOs, businesses, and communities work together.

Our Objectives

01

Education Support

Scholarships, learning materials, school fee assistance, and mentorship programmes that keep children in school and help adults access further education.

02

Empowerment Programmes

Vocational training, entrepreneurship workshops, financial literacy sessions, and livelihood support for individuals across all demographics.

03

Grant Disbursement to NGOs

Strategic funding for registered, credible NGOs whose missions align with education, health, poverty alleviation, and community development.
